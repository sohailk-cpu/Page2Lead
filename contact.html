Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to create a landing page?

Basic landing pages: 2-3 business days. Pro landing pages with copywriting and testing: 5-7 business days. Custom packages: 1-2 weeks depending on complexity. We also offer priority delivery options for urgent projects.

Do I need to pay upfront?

We require 50% upfront payment to begin work on your project. The remaining 50% is due upon completion before we deliver the final files. This ensures commitment from both sides and allows us to allocate resources to your project.

Can you redesign my existing landing page?

Yes! We often work with clients to redesign and optimize existing landing pages for better conversions. We'll analyze your current page, identify improvement areas, and create a more effective version that converts better.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes. Basic package includes 2 rounds of revisions. Pro package includes 4 rounds of revisions. We work until you're 100% satisfied with the result. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds can be arranged if needed.

What information do you need from me to start?

We need: 1) Your business details and target audience, 2) Your goals for the landing page, 3) Any existing branding guidelines, 4) Content/photos if available, 5) Examples of landing pages you like, 6) Information about your competition.
