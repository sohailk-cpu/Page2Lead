Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to create a landing page?

Basic landing pages: 2-3 days. Pro landing pages with copywriting and testing: 5-7 days. Custom packages: 1-2 weeks depending on complexity.

Do I need to pay upfront?

We require 50% upfront for all projects. The remaining 50% is due upon completion before final files are delivered.

Can you redesign my existing landing page?

Yes! We often work with clients to redesign and optimize existing landing pages for better conversions.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes. Basic package includes 2 rounds of revisions. Pro package includes 4 rounds of revisions. We work until you're happy with the result.

What information do you need from me to start?

We need: 1) Your business details, 2) Target audience information, 3) Your goals for the landing page, 4) Any branding guidelines, 5) Content/photos if available.
