About Page2Lead

A lead generation platform connecting customers with verified local service providers in Ajmer, Rajasthan.

What is Page2Lead?

Page2Lead is a lead generation platform based in Ajmer, Rajasthan. We help customers find verified local service providers quickly — and we help local service businesses get genuine customer leads.

We do not provide AC repair, plumbing, electrical, cleaning, or pest control services ourselves. We are the bridge between the customer and the right local professional.

In addition, Page2Lead also helps coaching institutes generate high-quality student leads across India and international markets using performance marketing funnels.

When a customer submits a request on our platform, we send that lead to a verified local provider in Ajmer or to a coaching institute. The provider or institute then contacts the customer directly.

🎯 Our Mission

To make it easy for people in Ajmer to find trusted local service providers — and to help businesses grow by connecting them with real customers searching online.
