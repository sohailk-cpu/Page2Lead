My Story

Why I Built Page2Lead

I started Page2Lead after noticing a frustrating pattern — coaching businesses with great teachers were struggling to find students, while less qualified institutes were growing simply because they had better marketing.

The problem wasn't the quality of teaching. It was that most coaching owners didn't know how to get consistent student enquiries. And the "solutions" out there — expensive agencies, complicated tools, overpriced packages — weren't built for someone running a coaching class or tutoring business.

"A good teacher deserves a full batch. The system I built is my way of making that happen — directly, simply, without unnecessary complications."

So I built a focused, WhatsApp-first lead system — targeted ads, a clean landing page, direct enquiries on your phone. No CRM. No dashboard to learn. Just students reaching you on WhatsApp.

I also build websites for coaching businesses — because having a good landing page matters. Whether you need just a website or a complete lead generation system, I handle it personally.

I work with a limited number of clients at a time. That's intentional — I'd rather do a small number of setups really well than spread thin across too many clients.
