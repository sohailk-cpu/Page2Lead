My Story

Why I Built Page2Lead

I started Page2Lead after noticing a frustrating pattern — coaching businesses with great teachers were struggling to find students, while less qualified institutes were growing simply because they had a better marketing setup.

The problem wasn't the quality of teaching. It was that most coaching owners didn't know how to get consistent student enquiries. And the "solutions" out there — agencies, complicated tools, overpriced packages — weren't built for someone running a coaching class.

"A good teacher deserves a full batch. The system I built is my way of making that happen — directly, simply, without unnecessary complications."

So I built a focused, WhatsApp-first lead system — targeted ads, a clean landing page, direct enquiries on WhatsApp. No CRM. No dashboard to learn. Just students reaching you on the same app you already use.

I also run a secondary service for local businesses in Ajmer — connecting people who need home services (AC repair, plumbing, electrical, cleaning, pest control) with verified local providers. It's a different model, but the same idea: direct connections, no unnecessary middlemen.

I work with a limited number of clients at a time. That's intentional — I'd rather do a small number of setups really well than spread thin across too many clients.
