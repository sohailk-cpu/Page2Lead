Privacy Policy

How I collect, use, and protect your information

📅 Last updated: March 2026

Summary: Page2Lead is a lead generation platform run by Sohail Khan. When you submit a request — whether for a home service in Ajmer or a coaching business enquiry — your contact details are used only to connect you with the relevant service provider or to follow up with you. I do not sell your data to third parties.

1. Who I Am

Page2Lead is run by Sohail Khan, a solo freelancer based in Ajmer, Rajasthan, India. I operate two services on this platform:

  • Coaching Lead Generation — I help coaching institutes, online tutors, and study abroad consultants get student enquiries through targeted ads and landing pages.
  • Local Home Services — Ajmer — I connect customers in Ajmer with verified local service providers for AC repair, plumbing, electrical, cleaning, and pest control.

You can contact me at: page2lead.contact@gmail.com or +91 75972 56642

2. Information I Collect

I collect the following information depending on how you use the platform:

  • From customers (home service requests): Name, phone number, service type, area in Ajmer, and problem description.
  • From coaching business enquiries: Name, phone number, coaching type, target location, ad budget range, and any message you send.
  • From service provider registrations: Name, business name, phone number, service type, and service area.
  • From contact form submissions: Name, phone number, email (optional), and message.

3. How I Use Your Information

I use the information I collect for the following purposes only:

  • To connect home service customers with a verified local provider in Ajmer.
  • To follow up on coaching business enquiries and schedule strategy calls.
  • To manage provider accounts and lead delivery on the platform.
  • To send you relevant updates related to your request or account.
  • To improve the platform and understand how people use it.

I do not use your information for any other purpose without your consent.

4. How I Share Your Information

I only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers (home services): When you submit a service request in Ajmer, your name, phone number, area, and problem are shared with the matched local provider so they can call you back.
  • For coaching clients: Your enquiry details are not shared with anyone — they are used only by me (Sohail Khan) to follow up with you directly.
  • With ad platforms: If I run ads for a coaching client, aggregated and anonymised data may be used within Google Ads or Meta Ads for targeting. No personal data is shared with these platforms beyond standard ad tracking.

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

5. Data Storage and Security

All submitted data is stored on a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway (railway.app), a reputable cloud infrastructure provider. I take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorised access or disclosure.

However, no system is completely secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact me immediately at page2lead.contact@gmail.com.

6. How Long I Keep Your Data

I keep your data only as long as necessary:

  • Customer lead data: retained for up to 12 months for follow-up and analytics.
  • Provider account data: retained while the account is active and for 6 months after.
  • Coaching enquiry data: retained until the enquiry is resolved or for up to 12 months.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting me.
