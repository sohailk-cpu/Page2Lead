Privacy Policy

How I collect, use and protect your information

📅 Last updated: March 2026

Summary: Page2Lead is a coaching lead generation and website building service run by Sohail Khan. When you submit an enquiry or contact me, your details are used only to follow up with you. I do not sell your data to third parties.

1. Who I Am

Page2Lead is run by Sohail Khan, a solo freelancer based in Ajmer, Rajasthan, India. I offer two services:

  • Coaching Lead Generation — I help coaching institutes, online tutors and study abroad consultants get student enquiries through targeted ads and WhatsApp-based systems.
  • Coaching Website Building — I build professional websites and landing pages for coaching businesses.

Contact: page2lead.contact@gmail.com  |  +91 75972 56642

2. What Information I Collect

I collect the following depending on how you use the platform:

  • From coaching business enquiries: Your name, phone number, coaching type, target location, budget range and any message you send.
  • From website enquiries: Your name, phone number, type of coaching business and project requirements.
  • From the contact form: Name, phone number, email (optional) and your message.
  • From coaching clients (students): If I run a lead campaign for a coaching client, students who submit enquiry forms provide their name, phone number and course interest. This data belongs to the coaching client and is delivered to them via WhatsApp.

3. How I Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide the service you requested.
  • To follow up on coaching or website project discussions.
  • To deliver student leads to coaching clients as part of the lead generation service.
  • To send relevant updates related to your project or account.
  • To improve the platform and understand how people use it.

I do not use your information for any other purpose without your consent.

4. How I Share Your Information

  • Student enquiries for coaching clients: When a student submits a form as part of a coaching client's ad campaign, their details (name, phone, course interest) are sent to that coaching client via WhatsApp. This is the core of the lead generation service.
  • Ad platforms: If I run ads for a coaching client, I use Google Ads or Meta Ads. Standard tracking pixels may be used. No personal data is shared with ad platforms beyond what these services normally collect.
  • No third-party selling: I do not sell, rent or trade any personal information to third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

Enquiry data submitted through the platform is stored on a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway (railway.app), a reputable cloud infrastructure provider. I take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorised access.

If you believe your data has been compromised, contact me immediately at page2lead.contact@gmail.com.

6. How Long I Keep Your Data

  • Coaching business enquiries: up to 12 months.
  • Student lead data (from coaching campaigns): up to 6 months after the campaign ends.
  • Website project enquiries: until the project is complete or for up to 12 months.

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting me.

7. Cookies

This website uses a basic cookie consent banner. I may use minimal analytics cookies to understand how people use the site. No third-party advertising cookies are placed beyond standard Google or Meta tracking pixels used for active ad campaigns.

  • Request deletion of your data.
