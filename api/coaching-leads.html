Frequently Asked

How quickly can I start getting student enquiries?

Setup typically takes 3–5 days — landing page, form, and ad campaign. Once the ad is approved and live, enquiries can start coming in within 24–48 hours.

What's the difference between management fee and ad budget?

Management fee is what you pay me to set up and manage the system. Ad budget is what you pay directly to Google or Meta to run your ads. I do not touch your ad budget — it goes directly to the platform.

Do I need any tech skills?

No. I handle the entire setup — landing page, form, ad account, tracking. You only need WhatsApp on your phone to receive leads.

Can you target students from specific countries like Australia or Canada?

Yes — the Standard and Advanced plans include international targeting. Meta Ads allows very precise location and interest targeting for study abroad and online coaching.

What if I'm not happy with the results?

Plans are month-to-month. If it's not working, you can stop. Before signing up, I do a free strategy call to check if this is realistic for your coaching business — I'll tell you honestly if I think it won't work.
