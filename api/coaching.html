Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

What exactly is the management fee vs ad budget?

Management fee is what you pay me for setting up and managing your lead system — landing page, ad campaign, optimisation, reports. Ad budget is separate and goes directly from you to Google or Meta. I don't handle your ad money and don't profit from it.

Can I start with the Basic plan and upgrade later?

Yes. Most clients start with Basic or Standard to test the system. If results are good and you want to scale — we upgrade. There's no penalty for upgrading or changing plans.

Do you guarantee a specific number of leads?

No — and I won't. Anyone who guarantees a specific number of leads is not being honest with you. Results depend on your ad budget, course demand, competition, and how quickly you follow up. I guarantee my full effort and transparency.

What happens if I want to stop?

You can stop any month with reasonable notice. The current month's management fee is non-refundable but there's no further obligation. The landing page I built can be handed over to you.

Is there a setup fee?

No separate setup fee. The first month's management fee covers the setup — landing page, ad account creation, campaign setup. Everything is included from day one.
